Swan Love is a creative collective

that organise fabulous events incorporating music, performers and artists for a truly entertaining experience. We bring together the raw talents from around the UK to build a network of entertainment professionals for any type of event.

We are always looking for affiliations with like-minded organisations and companies to sponsor various elements of our events.

Swan Love helps charities

Swan Love aims to organise two spectacular musical events every year. We work seamlessly together with local talent such as artists, DJs, musicians, dancers, promoters, venues, technology companies and voluntary workers. Our goal is to help support, raise awareness and fund raise for small local charities who are in need.
