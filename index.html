Swan Love is a creative collective

Swan Love is a team of talented individuals who organise fabulous events incorporating music, performers, artists, dance and media. The Swan Love collective harnesses the raw talents from around West Yorkshire to build a network of entertainment professionals for any type of event.

We are always looking for affiliations with like-minded organisations to sponsor various elements of our events.

On the 14th February 2014 we are fundraising for the children of Syria

Syria - 130,000 dead, 575,000+ injured, 2 million refugees outside Syria and 4 million displaced inside Syria with 1000s suffering starvation and lack of shelter. We've seen the pictures in the news, we've watched BBC Panorama's Children of Syria, we MUST do something to help. Every little counts.
Swan Love is fundraising for the children of Syria this Valentine's Day. On the most romantic day of the year we are urging you to come and share your love and show your love for a different reason - to raise money for Syria with the charity Hand in Hand for Syria.
The tickets are £10 and we promise you an evening of fun and entertainment with some of West Yorkshire's best artists, dancers and DJs.

DJ's - Jazz, Electro Swing and House.

  • 6pm-8pm ~ Art Exhibition ~ Cassandra Tainsh with Harpists Ruby Paul & Bernie
  • 8pm-8.45pm ~ Contemporary Dancers from Northern School of Contemporary Dance
  • 8.45-9pm ~ Burlesque showtime ~ Jennie Swift
  • 9pm-9.20 ~ Talk with Hand in Hand for Syria
  • 9.20-10.30 ~ DJ JD ~ Balearic tunes with Bernadette on Box
  • 10.30-11.30 ~ DJ Russthelovemust with Saxophone Stew
  • 11.30-12.30 ~ DJ Cat with Arran Kent on Sax
  • 12.30-2am ~ DJ Tim with electric violinist Jessica Crabtree
There will also be jugglers and magicians mingling with you so enjoy!
