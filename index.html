Swan Love is a creative collective

Swan Love use the power of music, dance, art and media to raise funds and awareness for great causes. Our aim is to continue to build a strong team of talented individuals with the goal of throwing the most beautiful, elegant and exciting events in fabulous venues. Swan Love will also develop a team to help commercial enterprise by managing unique corporate functions. We are always looking for affiliations with like minded companies for sponsorship to ensure our events are spectacular.

On the 14th February 2014 we are having our launch event in Leeds.

The venue: The White Cloth Gallery. We will be donating all of the profits from the night to a charity is called Hand in Hand for Syria.

DJ's - Jazz, Electro Swing and House.

Visuals - highlighting the charity and complementing the music.
Dancers - Northern School of Contemporary Dance.
Musicians - Saxophonist and Bongos.
Artists - who will auction their work for the cause.
Performers - Jugglers, a Magician & acrobats.
