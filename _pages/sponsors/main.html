Log Heights

The ultimate outdoor adventure

Regardless of your age or fitness level Log Heights ropes course is designed to challenge and thrill, you could be taking a trapeze jump, climbing a giant ladder or riding a zip wire. If you are looking for something exciting to do with friends, the experience at Log Heights is a must and will be talked about for years.

Supervised by expert technicians, their adrenaline fuelled ropes session makes a great day out. You will be amazed by what you can achieve!

Log Heights is located in the grounds of Ripley Castle, near Harrogate. Open during the week for corporate teambuilding activities and on weekends/school holidays to the public.
