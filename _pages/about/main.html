Angelo Mazzeo

Maz is the founder and creative director of Swan Love. With over 10 years events experience organising The Seaside Tribe parties a move to Leeds was not going to stop him. During the summer of 2013 he read a book by Trueman Capote called 'Party of the Century' This book inspired him to party again and Swan Love was born. Our aim is to attract the most beautiful and talented musicians, artists and dancers to party together to help great causes.

Baroness Guillem

Baroness Guillem is an experienced performer and choreographer with extensive ballet and tap skills which she transforms into burlesque routines. As an artist Baroness Guillem is in her element performing anything from a classical ballet influenced burlesque routine to a slap stick comedy clown act. The experience of the audience is paramount for this artist and they can expect to be taken on a journey which will leave them enthused and thoroughly entertained.
